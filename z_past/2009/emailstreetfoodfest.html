Dear Patrons,

Delfina is proud to be a part of the first San Francisco Street Food Festival on August 22nd.

As part of this wonderful event, Delfina has donated an experiential gift to the event's Silent Auction.

Please take a moment to check out the list of the incredible silent auction prizes. We hope you'll place a bid on us! Highlights of the auction include:

  • Chef for a Day at Chez Panisse
  • Dinner cooked for you at your home with Craig Stoll of Delfina
  • A chefs' weekend at the Ahwanee Resort
  • ...and more!

The auction will be live and online for 10 days starting August 17th. Many of the silent auction prizes are perfectly designed for large groups to share. So, get your group and get to bidding!

Find out more about the Silent Auction at www.sfstreetfoodfest.com

Be sure to join Delfina at the San Francisco Street Food Festival. We're partnering with La Cocina, sponsors like 7x7 Magazine, and many others to bring the tastiest street foods from around the world to the streets of San Francisco.

DATE: Saturday, August 22
TIME: 11 a.m. to 7 p.m.
LOCATION: Folsom Street between 25th and 26th, in front of La Cocina in San Francisco's Mission District

ADMISSION: Entrance is free. All food is priced between $3 and $8, and we'll have a host of cocktails, beer and wine all mixed by expert bartenders. Passport tickets (an easy way to save time and money) are on sale online so don't hesitate. Buy your tickets now and be a part of a great day.

La Cocina is a non-profit incubator kitchen in San Francisco that works to provide low-income and immigrant entrepreneurs with the resources they need to launch and grow successful food businesses. This festival will feature nine of La Cocina's vendors, eight restaurants and three informal vendors teamed with non-profits from around the city.
